Travel and Tours

20 years experience as a personal tour guide has given me a great deal of in-depth knowledge of this great island.

Once you have contacted me with your requirements, I take care of all your internal bookings and reservations before you arrive.

Then once you touchdown in Sri Lanka you can look forward to airport pickup, hotel transfers and your travel around the country in a clean, air-conditioned, comfortable vehicle.

My personally chauffeured tours have been highly commended by my past customers, especially for my safe driving - almost unique in Sri Lanka!!

The best sites and more
As well as visits to the regular historical tourist sites, I specialize in arranging schedules around your personal preferences. Whatever that may be:
hiking, trekking, kayaking, camping, fishing or wildlife and photo safaris.

Avoiding the large groups that others organize for these activities and with my years of experience, I know the best times to visit the sites and attractions ensuring that you have a stress-free and unforgettable trip.

Accomodation
With a large network of hotel and guest houses throughout the country you can be assured of a restful night after a days sight seeing and activities.

There are many 5-star establishments in Colombo and along the west coast beaches. Also a range of hotels, guest houses and rest houses (a quaint remnant of colonial rule) to fit all budgets.

Once I know your itinerary and preferences I can suggest the best places for you to stay. Be it after a days sightseeing or lounging on the beach, in a central location or a bit off the beaten track - but always safe and welcoming.
